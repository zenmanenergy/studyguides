Calculus 1 — Unit 8: Applications of Integration

Area Between Two Curves

\[ A = \int_a^b \bigl[f(x) - g(x)\bigr]\,dx \quad \text{where } f(x) \ge g(x) \text{ on } [a,b] \]
  • Find intersection points to determine limits of integration
  • Always subtract the lower curve from the upper curve (so the integrand is non-negative)
  • If curves cross on the interval, split into sub-intervals and add absolute values
  • Integrating with respect to \(y\): \(A = \int_c^d [\text{right} - \text{left}]\,dy\)

Average Value of a Function

\[ f_{\text{avg}} = \frac{1}{b-a} \int_a^b f(x)\,dx \]
  • Mean Value Theorem for Integrals: there exists \(c \in [a,b]\) such that \(f(c) = f_{\text{avg}}\)
  • Geometric interpretation: the rectangle with height \(f_{\text{avg}}\) and width \(b-a\) has the same area as under the curve

Displacement vs Total Distance

QuantityFormulaNotes
Displacement\(\displaystyle\int_a^b v(t)\,dt\)Net signed change in position
Total Distance\(\displaystyle\int_a^b |v(t)|\,dt\)Split at zeros of \(v(t)\)
Position\(s(t) = s(a) + \displaystyle\int_a^t v(u)\,du\)Accumulation from initial position
  • Displacement can be negative; total distance is always ≥ 0
  • To find total distance, find when \(v(t) = 0\), split integral, take absolute value of each piece

Accumulation and Net Change

\[ F(b) - F(a) = \int_a^b F'(x)\,dx \] Net change = integral of rate of change
  • If \(r(t)\) = rate of water flow, then \(\displaystyle\int_a^b r(t)\,dt\) = total water accumulated
  • If net change is positive, the quantity increased; if negative, it decreased
  • Read graphs of \(f'(x)\) to determine where \(f\) is increasing, decreasing, at a max/min

Volumes of Revolution (Disk/Washer)

Disk method (rotating about \(x\)-axis): \[ V = \pi \int_a^b [f(x)]^2\,dx \] Washer method (outer radius \(R\), inner radius \(r\)): \[ V = \pi \int_a^b \bigl[R(x)^2 - r(x)^2\bigr]\,dx \]
  • Shell method (rotating about \(y\)-axis): \(V = 2\pi \int_a^b x \cdot f(x)\,dx\)
  • Choose disk/washer vs shell based on which requires fewer sub-intervals
